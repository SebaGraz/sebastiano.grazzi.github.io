About Me

I am a Postdoc working in Computational Statistics. My research interests primary focus on the Theory and Methodology of Markov chain Monte Carlo. I have pushed forward the development of Monte Carlo methods driven by Piecewise deterministic Markov chains and I am currently developing parallel computations for Markov chains.
